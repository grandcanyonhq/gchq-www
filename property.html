The Property

A short drive from the southern entrance of the Grand Canyon, we encourage you to come explore our sprawling 80-acre ranch that offers unobstructed views of Red Butte and the San Francisco Peaks. On-site, some of the most popular activities include building campfires and stargazing using our telescope.

The Commons

Our Commons building, situated across from the four private casitas amongst swaying Juniper trees, is open to all guests during daytime hours. Appointed with custom artwork, furniture, and landscaping, you are welcome to explore the additional space for dining and lounging, enjoying a complimentary refreshment from the Canteen, as well as utilizing the outdoor patio for work or leisure purposes.
