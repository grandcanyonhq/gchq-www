Off-Grid

In managing expectations, please comply with the following requests for our remote retreat:

  • Electricity: Supplied by our solar panels. We kindly ask you to be mindful about your energy consumption, especially at night, and to leave electronic beauty and grooming devices at home. There is a backup generator.
  • Water: Our water (which is drinkable) is delivered weekly. Please limit showers to one quick shower per person/per day. We can source additional water on request.
  • Air Conditioner: Please remember to turn off the A/C every time you leave the casita and keep the sliding doors closed for dust and temperature regulation. Our staff will enter the unit if needed.
  • Heat: The casita has a wood burning stove with firewood and starter provided. Let us know if you need help.
  • Groceries: Can be found 30 minutes away in Williams, Tusayan, and Grand Canyon Village. There is a Whole Foods in Flagstaff and additional ice at the local gas station. We are a BYOB facility.
  • Cooking: Each casita has a mini fridge with freezer and a small gas stove, so you can prepare simple meals. Please don’t pour anything down the sink drain unless you would drink it.
  • Toilets: We kindly ask you to not put anything in the toilet other than human waste and the provided toilet paper.
  • Pets: Require pre-approval and owners are subject to additional cleaning fee.
  • Eco-Conscious: Refrain from using single-use plastics to the extent possible and check with us about starting campfires given frequent ‘Burn Bans’.
  • No Smoking
